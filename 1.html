What is Technology likely to be in 2020.

  • March 29, 2018
  • By Anish Gurung

Technology advancement has evolved so much from the past 10 years and totally changed the way people interact to the world.It has changed many aspects such as way of communicating, spending free times in your cell phones or ipads , way of how products are manufactured (using programmed robots) and many more.

I do remember when I was a kid, I used to watch people washing up their clothes in the veranda all day in such tampering sunlight rubbing the clothes and washing it again and again. But now I see those same people using microprocessed washing machines and are able to spend their time more efficiently.Not just washing machines, I see a total different world in front of me than what I used to see. Childrens and adults stick to their cell phones all day long. People rely on technology all the way from cooking food to washing the clothes.

Trending in Market Right now

Introduction to Robotics have immensely impacted people and their daily life. In fact even the introduction to new softwares available today effectively help doing works. Technology has been today a drug for people and even young generations starting off in their young school days start learning concepts on technology. People are so confident that this might take a huge role in the coming years.

With innovation and technology,seems we have forgotten to cherish the true beauty the world has to offer. ("A.C Van Cherub")

Teachers also prefer teaching students through the help of laptops or ipads to be more interactive session due to special animations and graphical learning giving a broader understanding.Social media platforms like facebook,snapchat,instagram are getting popular day by day simplyfying the means of communications.
